pause


A tool to encourage and practice self-care in a fast-paced world.

Life is fast, let's pause together.

In increasingly busy lives...

it has become more and more difficult to find time to take a moment, breathe, and practice self-care. Having originated from a perspective of spiritual navigation, Pause has evolved into a tool designed for travel and work intensive professionals to take a few seconds out of their day to connect with another individual around the globe and share a moment of self-reflection.
