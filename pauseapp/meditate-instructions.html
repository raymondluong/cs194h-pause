Mindfulness Tip

The body scan is a component of mindfulness meditation. It allows you to get in touch with the body, let go of feelings of needing to get stuff done, and release pent-up emotions. The body scan trains your mind to be able to move from detailed attention to a wider and more spacious awareness from one moment to the next.
