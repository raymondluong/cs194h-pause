Mindfulness...

is a mental state achieved by focusing one's awareness on the present moment, while calmly recognizing and accepting one's feelings, thoughts, and sensations.

The body scan is one part of mindfulness meditation and helps one let go of feelings of needing to get stuff done.

It trains the mind to move from detailed attention to a wider and more spatial awareness from one moment to the next.
